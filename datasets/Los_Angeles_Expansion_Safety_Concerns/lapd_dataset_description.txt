6. Los Angeles Expansion: Safety Concerns

Dataset:
LAPD Crime Data
https://data.lacity.org/Public-Safety/Crime-Data-from-2020-to-Present/2nrs-mtv8/about_data

Downloaded from website:
Crime_Data_from_2020_to_Present_20241004.csv

Motivation:
- A company is exploring the possibility of opening a new branch for its import/export business on the West Coast.
- The expansion would be a great opportunity to continue building and expanding the relationship with business partners in Asia.
- The goal is to find a prime warehousing location in the Los Angeles area, that has the lowest crime rate.
- The company wants to ensure the safety of its employees and of the business. 

Questions:
- Analyze all types of local crime in Los Angeles from 2020 - 2023.
- Determine what area would be the safest location.
- Determine what safeguards will be needed to ensure everyone's safety.
