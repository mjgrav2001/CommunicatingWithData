2. Last Stop Marketing Strategy - Covid 19 deaths:

US Dept of Health datasets:
https://catalog.data.gov/

Source:
https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/?q=Counts_of_Deaths_by_Select_Causes&sort=views_recent+desc&tags=deaths&ext_location=&ext_bbox=&ext_prev_extent=

Datasets:
- Monthly Counts of Deaths by Select Causes, 2014-2019 
- Monthly Provisional Counts of Deaths by Select Causes, 2020-2023

Extra datasets:
- Provisional COVID-19 Death Counts by Age in Years, 2020-2023
- Provisional COVID-19 Death Counts in the United States by County
- Conditions Contributing to COVID-19 Deaths, by State and Age, Provisional 2020-2023 
- Weekly Provisional Counts of Deaths by State and Select Causes, 2020-2023 
- Provisional Death Counts for Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19)
- Weekly Counts of Deaths by State and Select Causes, 2014-2019

Motivation:
- Funeral home operators
- Comprehensive end-of-life services, encompassing financial, legal, logistical, and emotional support
- Tailor marketing strategy based on insights into prevalent causes of death and related trends

Questions:
- With regards to the U.S. population, which chronic illnesses should we target based on their impact and trend?
- Should we adjust our marketing plan to account for seasonality?
- Due to COVID-19, were there any significant shifts in death trends regarding the selected chronic illnesses?

Assumptions:
- Chronic illnesses vary in their impact and trend within the U.S.population.
- Seasonal variations might influence the effectiveness of the marketing plan.
