
Datasets with descriptions:

12. Air Quality of City of New York

Dataset:
https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/air-quality

Environment and Health Data Portal: 
http://nyc.gov/health/environmentdata

Motivation:
- Air pollution is one of the most important environmental threats to urban populations.
- Pollutant emissions, levels of exposure, and population vulnerability vary across neighborhoods. 
- Exposures to common air pollutants have been linked to respiratory and cardiovascular diseases, cancers, and premature deaths. 
- These indicators provide a perspective across time and NYC geographies to better characterize air quality and health in NYC. 

Questions:
- Analyze the different types of air quality issues across NYC districts and across seasons
- Are there any correlations of different air quality issues across districts and seasons?
