
Datasets with descriptions:

11. Electric Vehicle Population Data

Metadata Updated: September 20, 2024

Dataset:
https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/electric-vehicle-population-data

This dataset shows the Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) that are currently registered through Washington State Department of Licensing (DOL).

Motivation:
Assess how the State of Washington can adjust its incentive structure to boost electric cars in specific counties.

Questions:
Analyze the distribution of electric vehicle types across State of Washington counties.
